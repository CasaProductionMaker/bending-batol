Block Game Wiki

Blocks

There are a ton of blocks in Block Game. Blocks are tiles that make up the world. They are placeable and destroyable, and some have special qualities.

Items

Items are things in the world that can enter your inventory. Blocks have an item version that you collect upon breaking the block, but there are other items that you cannot place down.

PvP

PvP in Block Game is sort of complicated. There are attack timings, RNG, and more.

Structures

Structures generate randomly in the Block Game world, overriding blocks and can contain loot chests or other types of treasure.

Crafting Recipes

Even though the Block Game world is plentiful, there are even more items you can acquire through crafting multiple items together.

Commands

Commands are a way for Admins to edit the world or do things not possible in the regular game.

Biomes

Biomes are different sections of the world that have different generation. For example, deserts have their floor made of sand instead of grass.
