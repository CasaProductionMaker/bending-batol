Kids Club Hub - Operations

Here, all the Kids Club Operations will be posted. Operations are plans to do something fun, useful, or any plans that have a codename, and are planned over a long stretch of time. Anyone can create operations, and delete their own operations. If you are interested in participating in an operation, press the "I'm In" button on the operation.
